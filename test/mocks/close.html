    How to use the Close votes queue
    Awarded at 3k reputation

    If a question is off topic for this site, lacks sufficient detail, or needs to be narrowed in scope, it may need to be closed. Questions other users have flagged for closure will appear in this queue.

    You can vote to Close, Edit, or Leave open.

    How to work through this queue:

    • Most posts require 3 votes to close them.
    • If enough people vote to Leave open, the question leaves the Close votes queue.
    • Consider using filters to find posts relevant to your interests and subject-matter expertise.
    • Skip the task if you aren't sure which action to take.
    Read more about this queue in our help center.
